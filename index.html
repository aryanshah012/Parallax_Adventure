ADVENTURE

Adventure Time

Apart from its breathtakingly beautiful landscapes, Interlaken is also a paradise for all adventure sports enthusiasts. In fact, the list of these thrill-inducing activities is quite unending when it comes to this resort town and is therefore the top Swiss destination for adventure sports. Get your adrenaline pumping as you bungee jump into the unknown. Experience your heart racing with excitement as you go sky diving above the massive mountains.

Nothing will stop you from channelling your inner Tiger Woods with a nice session of golf and once you’re done, you can explore the charming streets of the town on a Segway! And if you still haven’t had enough, Interlaken also has a kart track of its own for you to go wild.

PARA-GLIDING

Paragliding is a sport in which the players fly in the air using paragliders. These paragliders are light in weight and are foot launched. There is a harness in the glider on which the paraglider sits. This harness is interconnected to the glider with baffled cells.

Paragliding is an extreme sport that requires daring and a strong heart to play. A player has to fly above a huge height from the ground and needs to show his/her skill. Though the content of this tutorial is not broad enough to cover all the aspects of paragliding, it has sufficient details to supplement an unknown user with necessary details.

SURFING

I loved being surrounded in the translucent ocean water, so I decided I wanted to surf. My dad was kind enough to rent me a surfboard for the week, so i could learn how to surf. The day had finally come, and I was very eager to begin.

The sport of surfing provides opportunities for both relaxation and excitement, as athletes can enjoy the beauty of the ocean while also seeking out challenging waves to ride.
